What Is Ghostladder?

Ghostladder is a real-time price guide for ghostwrite toys. We track prices across every 100% blind box set. We build statistical estimates where direct sales data is thin. We calculate ROI based on the freshest data and most advanced models. We surface patterns that would take hours of manual research to find on your own. We're Campless powered by Claude.

This is a major problem when trying to calculate blind box ROI based on sales data alone.

The core number behind case ROI is Avg Hit Value — the population-weighted average price across all non-Base variants in the set. It answers: if I pull a guaranteed hit, what's it worth on average?

But if we don't have data on the rarest hits, we have to drop them from the calculation. The result is an Avg Hit Value that's systematically too low.

How We Fill the Gaps

When a card has no recent sales, we don't guess. We infer from structured relationships already in the data — variant ratios within a player, cross-player comps within a set, and cross-set benchmarks for structurally similar cards.

Each estimate is built from a weighted pool of reference points. Same-player comps carry the most weight. Same-variant comps from other players carry less. Cross-set signals carry the least.

Our estimation engine gives us an accurate expected case value. But averages hide variance. Just because the expected hit value is $312 doesn't mean your next case will be worth $312. In fact, most won't be — and a few will be worth 10x more.

That's why we built the CASE SIMULATOR, letting you see how ROI plays out across several — or even hundreds — of cases.
