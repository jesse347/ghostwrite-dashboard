Methodology: Each player index tracks all ghostwrite figures for that player across every active set and variant tier. The index uses an equal-weighted chained-return methodology — normalized to 1,000 at the date of the player's first recorded sale. Every card contributes its percentage return equally: a 10% move in a Fire counts the same as a 10% move in a Base. This treats each card as an equal signal of a player's market direction regardless of absolute price. New cards enter after a warm-up period (averaged over their first few sales) to prevent a single outlier sale from anchoring the baseline. Cards with no sale in the past 180 days are excluded from return calculations. A card is flagged stale in the table when its last sale is older than 60 days. All prices sourced from verified eBay completed sales.
