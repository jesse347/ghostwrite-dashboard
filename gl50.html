Methodology: The GL50 tracks 50 hand-curated ghostwrite figures across all active sets and variant tiers. The index uses a price-weighted chained-return methodology, normalized to 1,000 at the date of the first recorded sale. Each component's influence is proportional to the square root of its price — so a Fire at $10,000 carries significantly more weight than a Base at $80, but no single card can dominate the index entirely. This reflects the reality that rare, high-value cards are more meaningful signals of market health than cheap, liquid ones. A component's last-sold price carries forward until that component next sells. Components whose last sale is older than 60 days are flagged as stale. The component list may be updated at the editorial discretion of the ghostladder team. All prices sourced from verified eBay completed sales.
